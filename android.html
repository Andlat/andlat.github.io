Day 'n' Night

Android based video game (with custom game engine) where the goal is to kill teddy bears during your nightmares and collect items during the day throughout the city.

This project was done in close collaboration with 5 other students as part of our final project in the Cégep program Sciences Informatiques et Mathématiques

Technologies Used: OpenGL ES 3.0, Blender, Google Maps API, Java Android
